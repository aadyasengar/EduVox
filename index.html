🚀 Getting Started

  1. Add Subjects: Head to the Dashboard and add your current subjects (e.g., DBMS, Physics).
  2. Start Chatting: Use the Chat page to ask questions about topics within those subjects.
  3. Save Knowledge: Clip important explanations directly to your Notebook.

🗣️ Voice Commands

EduVox is designed for hands-free learning. Try these natural commands:

"Explain DBMS"
"Give notes on Operating Systems"
"Start quiz on DSA"

✨ Features Overview

🧠 Adaptive Chat

Learns your style. Whether you prefer bullet points or simple analogies, the AI adjusts to your preference.

📝 Smart Notebook

Auto-organizes clips from chat into subject-based notebooks. Includes tagging and pinned notes.

🧪 AI Quizzes

Generate tests on the fly. Earn XP, track accuracy, and level up your academic rank.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I earn XP?

You earn XP by correctly answering quiz questions, completing study sessions, and saving high-quality notes.

Can I export my notes?

Yes! Go to Settings > Data and use the "Download Save Data" option to get a JSON backup of everything.

Is my data private?

EduVox stores all your data locally on your device. We do not transmit your notes or personal info to external servers.
