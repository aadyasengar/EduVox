# 🎓 EduVox: Your Advanced AI Study Buddy

EduVox is a next-generation, AI-powered educational platform designed to provide a highly personalized, hands-free, and gamified learning experience. It combines behavioral modeling, automated tutoring, and real-time voice interaction to help students master any subject with ease.

## 🌟 The Vision & Purpose
Most digital learning today is **passive**—students watch videos or read notes without truly absorbing the material. EduVox was built to solve this by:
- **Transforming Passive into Active**: Using "Teach-Back" mechanics and "Doubt Detection" to ensure students aren't just reading, but understanding.
- **Eliminating Decision Fatigue**: The "Study Autopilot" takes the burden of "what to study" away, guiding students through a scientific learning flow.
- **Emotional & Behavioral Connection**: The "Digital Twin" system makes the AI feel like a personal tutor that knows your strengths, celebrates your wins, and helps you navigate your struggles.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 🧬 Digital Twin Learner
EduVox creates a "Digital Twin" of your learning persona. It tracks mastery trends, engagement styles, and performance predictions to tailor every session to you.

### 🤖 Study Autopilot Mode
A fully guided, hands-free learning experience. The AI leads you through a complete cycle: Explanation → Active Recall (Teach-Back) → Quiz → Session Summary.

### ⚔️ Boss Battle Mode
Gamify your progress with high-stakes challenges. Unlocks at 80% subject progress, featuring a 60-second boss fight with dynamic HP tracking.

### 📋 Academic Intelligence
- **Auto Report Cards**: Instantly generate professional academic reports.
- **Session Replay**: Review your learning journey in a structured timeline.
- **Mistake Engine**: Deep conceptual explanations for every wrong answer.
- **AI Doubt Detection**: Automatically simplifies concepts (ELI5) when you struggle.

## 🛠️ Technology Stack
- **Frontend**: Vite + HTML5 + CSS3 (Modern Glassmorphic Design)
- **Logic**: Vanilla JavaScript (Module Architecture)
- **State Management**: Persistant LocalStorage with `StorageService`.
- **Speech**: Web Speech API (Recognition & Synthesis).
